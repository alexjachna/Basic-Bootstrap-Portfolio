Alex Jachna

3rd Year CCIT Student at UTM

Hi! I'm Alex, and I'm a CCIT student at the University of Toronto, in hopes of becoming a UX/UI designer. I love anything related to mountains or hiking, as well as its aesthetic, so I always try to implement my passion of nature into my projects.

Ever Since I started programming, I fell in love with the challenge, but also the freedom to be able to create what you desire. With this in mind, I prefer the front-end work of things, rather than the technical side of programming.

Creativity

Design
