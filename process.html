The Process.

inspirations and plans behind the website.

Inspirations

Since the entire objective of this website was to strictly base it off bootstrap elements and components, I tried to recieve inspirations from many templates, but more specifically, successful templates that actually are visually appealing. I felt like in order to succeed, each page had to include a minimal amount of content, rather than a lot.


Design Plans

Banners

I am a big fan with viewport banners, since it allows websites to display one image of what you want your entire website to be about. In this case, since I love nature, I wanted to display some image that captures that idea, and display text and navigation.

Profile Picture

I feel like a rounded profile picture with a minimal amount of text describing the image is most effective, because it allows the users who are viewing the website to quickly understand the purpose of the page.

Navigations

The navigations behind this website were made to be as simplistic as possible. Having a complicated navbar makes users think, which surprisingly is what you do NOT want. You want your users to freely be able to navigate around the website with ease.

Color Palette

I felt that having a basis for your color palette is key, and letting your images create its own environment for each page, but having simplistic colours and shades are important too. I feel like for professional websites, the 3 basic shades are typically: White, Black, and grey, as it allows for users to not get blinded by any other bright colours.
